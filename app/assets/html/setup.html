Device Setup
Connect your Sláinte dispenser to your home Wi-Fi.
1
Connect to your dispenser
On your phone, go to Settings → Wi-Fi and connect to:
Slainte_Setup
Then come back to this screen and continue below.
2
Enter your dispenser details

Enter the ID printed on the bottom of your dispenser, and the Wi-Fi network you want it to connect to.

Found on the label on the bottom of your device.
All set!
Your dispenser is connecting to your network.
This may take a few seconds.
